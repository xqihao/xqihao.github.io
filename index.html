Qi Hao

Associate Professor of Geophysics

Jilin University

Welcome to my homepage! My name is Qi Hao, the pronunciation of which is quite similar to that of Chee Haw as told by an Australian friend many years ago. a research scientist (at the assistant professor level) at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Dhahran, Saudi Arabia. After earning my Ph.D degree, I taught as a lecturer shortly at China University of Petroleum, Qingdao, China, and then worked as a postdoctoral researcher of Geophysics at Norwegian University of Science and Technology, Trondheim, Norway. I was academically advised by professors Deli Wang (JLU), Qiaodeng He (JLU), Jiwen Teng (IGGCAS), Alexey Stovas (NTNU), Tariq Alkhalifah (KAUST), Stewart Greenhalgh (ETH Zurich) and Peter Mora (KFUPM).
